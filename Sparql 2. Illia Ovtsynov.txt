QUESTION 1
What license does the <http://resources.samsung.com/projects/.net> project have?

SPARQL QUERY 1
PREFIX terms: <http://purl.org/dc/terms/>
SELECT ?license
WHERE {<http://resources.samsung.com/projects/.net> <http://purl.org/dc/terms/license> ?license.
}
